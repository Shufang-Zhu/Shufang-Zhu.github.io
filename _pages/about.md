I am a Research Associate at the <a href="http://www.diag.uniroma1.it/">Dipartimento di Ingegneria Informatica, Automatica e Gestionale</a>. <a href="https://www.uniroma1.it/it/">Sapienza University of Rome</a>, working with <a href="http://www.diag.uniroma1.it//degiacom/">Prof. Giuseppe De Giacomo</a> on his Advanced ERC project <a href="https://whitemech.github.io/">WhiteMech</a>.

I received my Ph.D. degree in 2020, at <a href="http://english.ecnu.edu.cn/">East China Normal University (ECNU)</a>, Shanghai, China, under the supervision of <a href="https://faculty.ecnu.edu.cn/pgg_en">Prof. Geguang Pu</a>. 

During my Ph.D., I got the scholarship from <a href="https://www.chinesescholarshipcouncil.com/">Chinese Scholarship Council (CSC)</a> and studied as a visiting Ph.D. student (August 2016 to Feb 2018) at <a href="https://www.rice.edu/">Rice University</a>, under the supervision of <a href="https://www.cs.rice.edu/~vardi/">Prof. Moshe Y. Vardi</a>.


My main interests are in automated reasoning and formal verification.
